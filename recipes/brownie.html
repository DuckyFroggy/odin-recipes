Description

A classic dessert item

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs and Water
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Unsweetend cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients in two seperate bowls.
  2. Next, combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  3. Pour batter into an 8x8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake!
